# Moveset Formulas 

## DamageMove
- OutDamage: 20 + FightMon.attack 

## Damage taken from DamageMove
- remainingHealth: FightMon.health - (OutDamage - FightMon.defense)

## DefenseMove
- This move has speed priority, regardless of players speed. 

## BuffMove
- BuffAttack: FightMon.attack + (FightMon.attack * (0.5))
- BuffSpeed: FightMon.speed + (FightMon.speed * (0.5))
- BuffDefense: FightMon.defense + (FightMon.defense * (0.5))

## BuffMove
- DeBuffAttack: OpponentFightMon.attack - (FightMon.attack * (0.5))
- DeBuffSpeed: OpponentFightMon.speed - (FightMon.speed * (0.5))
- DeBuffDefense: OpponentFightMon.defense - (FightMon.defense * (0.5))
